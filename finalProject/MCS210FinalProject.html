Final Project

MCS 210

Fall 2023

Presentations will be the last day(s) of class, 11 & 12 December


For this project, you will design a web site to inform or persuade a viewer on a topic of your choice.  The topic you choose must be at least tangentially related to this class.

To be related to the class, it must deal with a social, ethical or moral issue and technology.  Examples of ideas that I may use if I was doing this project:

    Technology and our betting culture for sports.  Are we using technology to target people at risk of chronic gambling through sports betting?

    E sports, good or bad?  How is the prevalence of E sports impacting our youth?  Are we encouraging socialization and activity or dampening it?

    How can we regulate that which we do not understand?  What level of technical literacy do our lawmakers have?  What about the general public?
